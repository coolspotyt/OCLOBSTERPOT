Catch № 06 — Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours?

We're open daily from 11 AM to 10 PM.

Are you open year-round?

We're open seasonally, mid-March through October 31st.

Indoor or outdoor seating?

We offer indoor seating.

Do you deliver?

Yes — find us on Grubhub and Uber Eats.

Do you accommodate food allergies?

Of course. Tell your server about any allergies — we always ask and accommodate carefully.

Vegetarian or non-seafood options?

Yes, we have vegetarian and non-seafood options.

Can I customize my order?

We prefer to avoid customizations to keep quality and consistency high.

Is there parking nearby?

We're right on the boardwalk — parking is available around town.

Are pets allowed?

No pets, due to possible customer allergies.

Private events or catering?

Absolutely — email info@oclobsterpot.com to make arrangements.

About Us

Welcome to OC Lobster Pot — Ocean City’s New Seafood Spot. We’re the new kids on the boardwalk, but we’re bringing big flavor and even bigger heart. Born from a love for bold lobster rolls, fresh coastal bites, and that laid-back Ocean City vibe, OC Lobster Pot is here to shake up your shore eats. Whether you’re grabbing a roll to-go, diving into seafood fries, or sipping something cold with friends, we’re all about good food, good people, and good times by the beach. Come taste what’s next.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?

We are open daily from 11 AM to 10 PM.

Do you offer indoor or outdoor seating?

We offer indoor seating only.

Are you open year-round?

We are open seasonally from mid-March through October 31st (Halloween).

Can I customize my order?

We prefer to avoid customizations to maintain consistency and quality.

Is there parking nearby?

We're located right on the boardwalk, so parking can be found around town!

Do you accommodate food allergies?

Of course! Please inform your server of any allergies — we always ask and accommodate carefully.

Do you have vegetarian or non-seafood options?

Yes, we do offer vegetarian and non-seafood options.

Are pets allowed?

No, pets are not allowed due to possible customer allergies.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we deliver through Grubhub and Uber Eats.

Can I host a private event or group?

Absolutely! We cater — please contact us at info@oclobsterpot.com to make arrangements.
